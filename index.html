About SRI-HITECH-INFRA

A growing construction company committed to reliable execution and long-term relationships.

Who We Are

SRI-HITECH-INFRA is a construction and infrastructure firm specializing in residential, commercial, and small-scale industrial projects. We focus on practical designs, cost-effective execution, and high-quality workmanship.

From initial planning and estimating to final handover, we work closely with clients, architects, and engineers to ensure each project is delivered as per agreed timelines and specifications.

What We Value
  • Transparency: Clear communication on scope, cost, and timelines.
  • Quality: Proper materials, trained workforce, and site supervision.
  • Safety: Safe work practices and compliance with site norms.
  • Commitment: We treat every project as a long-term relationship, not a one-time job.
